## Experiments / 实验部分

### Exp 1: Current and Circuit Experiment
### 实验一：电流和电路实验

![Exp 1-2 Diagrams](images/Exp1.png)

**Objective / 目标:** Understand how a circuit forms.

**Procedure / 步骤:**
1. Connect the Electronic Module shown in the Picture and turn the knife switch to **ON**.
2. The LED indicator would light up — this is the basic functioning state.
3. Remove one module, the LED would stop lighting.

**Key principle / 简要原理:** Circuits must need current flowing in it.

> **ZH:** 接按图将电子模块接上电子模块，开关开到“ON” LED灯会亮。通过实验知道，当电子模块一个一个插入时，每次插入后 LED灯越亮。实验中电子模块的数量越多，通过LED的电流越大。

### Exp 2: Directionality of Current & Rectification
### 实验二：电路方向性和整流作用实验

![Exp 1-2 Diagrams](images/Ex2.png)
**Objective / 目标:** Understand how a diode controls current direction.

**Procedure / 步骤:**
1. Turn knife switch to **ON**; LED lights.
2. Insert a diode in forward orientation → LED stays on.
3. Reverse the diode 180° → LED turns off.
4. Conclusion: diodes only allow current in **one direction**.

> **ZH:** 接按图，开关到“ON” LED灯会亮。按图方向将二极管接入+一极电路，LED灯亮。将二极管调换方向，LED不亮。说明电流只能沿一个方向通过二极管。

---

### Exp 3: Semiconductor Characteristics
### 实验三：电晶体的特性实验


![Exp 3-6 Diagrams](images/Exp3.png)

**Objective / 目标:** Explore transistor (NPN) current amplification.

**Procedure / 步骤:**
1. Turn to **ON**. Without base connection → LED off.
2. Connect large resistor to base → LED glows faintly.
3. Replace with smaller resistor → LED brightens significantly.
4. Conclusion: **transistors amplify current**.

> **ZH:** 接按图，开关开到“ON”，电路连接后，LED灯仍然不亮。当接上基极大电阵时，LED灯微微发亮。当换上小一些阻値的电阵时，LED灯变得较亮。来分析结论：晶体管有放大电流的功能。

### Exp 4: Brightness Control Circuit
### 实验四：灯亮度控制电路实验

![Exp 1-2 Diagrams](images/Exp4.png)
**Objective / 目标:** Use a potentiometer to control LED brightness via a transistor.

**Procedure / 步骤:**
1. Connect circuit with 20kΩ resistor and LED via transistor. Turn to **ON**.
2. Rotate the potentiometer: **more resistance → LED dims**; **less resistance → LED brightens**.

> **ZH:** 接按图，开关到“ON”。通过改变可变电阵的阻値，LED亮度可以发生变化。阻値越大，LED越暗；阻値越小，LED越亮。

### 实验五：导体和络缘体实验

![Exp 1-2 Diagrams](images/Exp5.png)

**Procedure / 步骤:**

## Basic Principles / 基本原理

![Basic Principles](images/s09_basic_principles.jpg)

### What is a Circuit? / 什么是电路？

An **electric circuit** is a closed loop through which electric current can flow, consisting of:

| Component | 中文 | Role |
|-----------|---------|------|
| Power source | 电源 | Provides voltage (EMF) |
| Conductor | 导线 | Carries current |
| Load | 负载 (灯、电机等) | Converts electrical energy |
| Switch | 开关 | Controls circuit on/off |

### Key Components / 元件工作原理

- **Diode (二极管):** One-directional current flow; used for rectification.
- **Transistor (三极管/晶体管):** Small base current controls large collector current — amplification & switching.
- **Capacitor (电容器):** Stores charge; creates time delays and filters signals.
- **Photoresistor (光敏电阴):** Resistance decreases as light increases — light sensing.
- **Electret Microphone (驻极体话筒):** Converts sound to electrical signals.

## Safety Notices / 安全注意事项

![Safety](images/s11_safety.jpg)

> ⚠️ **Please read all safety information before use / 使用前请阅读所有安全信息**

1. Always verify connections are correct before powering on.
2. Follow circuit diagrams exactly. Do not exceed rated voltages.
3. Keep metal tools away from battery contacts.
4. Do not put batteries in mouth. Store in a cool, dry place.
5. Use under adult supervision for children under 8.

> 🚨 **警告 / WARNING**
>
> Non-rechargeable batteries must **not** be recharged. Do not mix old and new batteries or different battery types. Always remove batteries when not in use. This product uses **AA × 3**.
>
> **ZH:** 非充电电池不能充电；充电电池在成人监护下充电；不同类型或新旧电池不得混用；不使用时应先取出电池；电池应正确插入电极性（+/−）；本产品使用“AA”电池 3节。
